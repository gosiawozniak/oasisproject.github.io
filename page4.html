ART BY ME

In the cozy ceramics café at Vesterbro Torv, you can let your creativity loose and paint exactly the type of ceramic and design that you want. Everyone can try, since the staff is supportive and helpful throughout the whole process, from when you draw your first line on the bowl to when you’re done and it needs to be burned.
Ceramics and painting
Art by me has over 200 different types of ceramics that you can pick between, 80 pretty colors and many different kind of techniques you can use, such as painting with soap bubbles or using a toothbrush.

Opening Hours

Monday - Friday: 10:30 - 22:00
Saturday - Sunday: 9:30 - 18:00

Price range: 125DKK - 549DKK

LOCATION

The Art by me in Aarhus Is located on Vesterbro Torv 6, 4B, 8000 Aarhus, almost right next to the Old town. Otherwise it can be found only a short walk away from either the main shopping street or the botanical garden.

Adress:Vesterbro Torv 4B, 8000 Aarhus
