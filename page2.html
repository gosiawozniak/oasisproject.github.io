STAND-UP AT ALBERT'S

Mostly a cocktail bar, Alberts has a rich history and so does the building. Located in an old monk basement from around the 1300s and named after the wine salesman Albert Thorup who ran a wine store in the same basement from 1885, Alberts has been there since 2015. Here you will be served quality cocktails and special beers and a few times a week be offered free entertainment such as stand-up comedy.

Opening Hours

Sunday: 19:30-21:30 (English)

Free

LOCATION

Located on Store torv 3, 8000 Aarhus, Alberts is right in the heart of the city. Right by the Aarhus theater, Aarhus cathedral and the viking museum, Alberts is very easy to find. Simply locate Store torv, which is a side street to the main shopping street, afterwards Alberts will be right on your left side of the street.

Adress: Store torv 3, 8000 Aarhuss
