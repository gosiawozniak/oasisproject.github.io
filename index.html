EXPLORE YOUR
CREATIVITY

Looking to explore your creativity or just find local gems and secrets around Aarhus? Have a hidden talent for drawing, a big love for plants or maybe just looking for a new hobby or place to go? Sort through our list of interesting places and you might just find exactly what you’re looking for. Unsure of what you want? Try your luck and generate a random place you can go to, alone or with your friends. On the site we offer a broad selection of everything from comedy if you just want to have a laugh and a drink, to workshops where you can express yourself in different ways and discover new talents you never knew you had.

ART BY ME

Like to paint? Love pretty bowls and vases? Look no further than this. At Art by me you can not only look at a big selection of handmade ceramics. Even better.. You can get to create your own, with no limitations or rules. Want to make a pretty bowl as a Christmas gift, or just a nice vase for your living room? Art by me is your place to go.

PLANTCAFEEN

Plantecafeen has something for everyone. A showroom and shop combined with a million different types of plants and décor, combined with a cozy café. With the showroom flooded with plants, you almost feel like you walk into a forest.

See More

STAND-UP AT ALBERT'S

Located right in the center of Aarhus, right next to the Aarhus cathedral, you will find Albert’s. Originally a cocktail bar, they offer more than just a great variety of drinks. Once a week in English, 4 times a week in Danish, you can go to Albert’s and laugh till your stomach hurts, all of this for free.
