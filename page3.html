PLANTCAFEEN

The plant café, or in Danish, “Plantecaféen”, is a botanical oasis located in the egg packery by Godsbanen, a cultural center. The café is located right between the plant store and the orangery and there is a subtle meditative vibe inside. Here you can enjoy small, delicious dishes and drinks. You can get sodas, wine and beers and when the weather is a bit colder, coffee, tea, hot chocolate and glogg.

Opening Hours

9:00am - 6:00pm
almost every day

LOCATION

Located at the egg packery by Godsbanen, the plant café is right in the middle of “Ceresbyen” and the Aros art museum. Found on the address Kjeld Tolstrups Gade 12, 8000 Aarhus, It can be a little tricky finding the entrance on your first trip there. Once you walk along the building, you will find an alley by the corner of the street, walk through this alley and you will see a building nicely decorated in flowers and plants, if you see this you’ve made it to the right place.
